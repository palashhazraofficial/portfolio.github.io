AI MEET
PROBLEM
We often forget to take notes during online meetings or classes. Or we take messy onces that we can't understand later. Sometimes we don't understand what are the key points or given tasks.
SOLUTION
AI MEET is a desktop application that records your meetings and then transcribe and summarized it with key points and action items. The AI remembers everything and can give you answers by analyzing multiple meetings. You can intregate it with Google Meet, Zoom, MS Teams, YouTube videos or normal video calls.
