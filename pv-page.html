Polyglot VoiceCode
PROBLEM
To less our workload, many of us use voice typing tools. Where mobile phones allows us to voice type, computers normally don't. We may use third-party applications on our PCs. But its need an costly subscribtion fee and an active internet connection.
SOLUTION
Polyglot VoiceCode is an offline voice typing tool especially design for Windows 10/11 PCs. And the great part is, it's avalible in 10+ languages including Hindi, Tamil, Arabic, Japanese, French and more. So type in your regional language, not only in 'English', without sharing your 'voice-data' to clouds.
